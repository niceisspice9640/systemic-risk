HHI measures the structural concentration of cloud infrastructure across the Big Three providers — a proxy for systemic blast radius when a single vendor fails.

HHI formula: Σ(s²) where s = each provider's market share in whole-number percentage points. Big Three only — full-market HHI would be lower in absolute value but follows the same directional trend.
†2024 reclassification: Synergy Research Group reclassified a portion of Azure revenues from IaaS/PaaS to SaaS in their 2024 reporting, producing an apparent drop in Azure share from 24% (2023) to 21% (2024) despite continued absolute revenue growth. The 2024 HHI value (1,485) reflects this methodological artifact rather than a genuine reversal in market concentration. The primary trend is treated as 2017–2023 (+30.7% HHI).
Sources: Synergy Research Group press releases (primary, 2022–2024 confirmed directly); Canalys Q4 2017 (GCP baseline); holori.com (Synergy-citing compilation, 2017–2023 cross-check). 2018–2021 individual shares interpolated from Synergy's stated annual rate-of-change (Azure +~2 pp/yr, GCP +~1 pp/yr) per Q1 2022 press release.
